Understanding httpbin Output

The Output will be in an argument list at the top of the httpbin page and will give it in alphabetical order, not the order of the form.

However, at the bottom of the page is also an output given as a single line in the order of the form seperated by &s.

Either of these outputs can be used when parsed later
